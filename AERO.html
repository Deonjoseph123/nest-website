NeST Digital Aerospace Program

NeST Digital Aerospace Program focuses on cutting-edge engineering skills required in the aviation and aerospace industries. Our curriculum is designed to prepare learners for advanced roles in aircraft systems, avionics, simulation engineering, and aerospace electronics.

With emerging demands in global aerospace technology, our training emphasizes precision engineering, safety compliance, real-time systems, embedded avionics, and digital engineering workflows followed by aerospace leaders worldwide.
