NeST Digital SFO-Dematic

The SFO-Dematic program at NeST Digital is designed to equip learners with expertise in warehouse automation, supply-chain robotics, digital logistics, and smart factory operations. As global industries shift toward Industry 4.0, SFO-Dematic training prepares professionals to excel in automation-driven environments.

This program emphasizes hands-on training in automated material handling systems, robotics integration, control systems, IoT-based monitoring, and advanced warehouse solutions. Learners gain real-world exposure to Dematic-style automation workflows used in modern logistics hubs.
